Etch-a-Sketch

Oh, The Nostalgia

Picture it now, it's the early 90's. You're waking up on the weekend, throw some Pop-tarts in the toaster and turn on Cartoon Network to watch your favorite show. You see a new toy called Etch-a-Sketch. A red box with knobs you can twist to draw stuff! Crazy!

Etch A Sketch is a great screen alternative -- no charging, batteries or Wi-Fi needed. Turn the knobs and lines will magically appear on the screen! Unplug with the Classic!

How it works

This Etch-a-Sketch takes a spin on the original concept of the game. Instead of spinning knobs to create a picture, we'll simply hover over the squares below with our mouse to start drawing! Let's get after it!
